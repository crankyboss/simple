Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 6th May 2025 | Last Updated: 6th May 2025

1. Introduction

hypeqraft ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and safeguard information when you use our multi-platform video management and analytics system ("Service").

By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy and our Terms and Services Agreement.

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information (Provided by Users)

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Account credentials (username, password)
  • Payment information for subscriptions or transactions

B. Technical & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, and browser version
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Platform usage statistics and session logs

C. Content & Analytical Data

  • Uploaded videos and metadata
  • Performance metrics from integrated third-party platforms
  • Aggregated insights for video analytics

D. Cookies & Automated Data Collection

  • Cookies: Used for user preferences, session tracking, and analytics.
  • Behavioral Analytics: Automated analysis of platform interactions for optimization.
  • Third-Party Tracking Tools: Used for performance and engagement insights.

Users can manage or disable cookies via browser settings or platform privacy preferences.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Service Functionality – Managing video uploads, analytics, and integrations.
  • User Authentication & Security – Protecting accounts against unauthorized activities.
  • Platform Optimization – Enhancing experience via behavioral insights.
  • Legal Compliance – Adhering to privacy laws and regulatory requirements.

4. Automated Data Processing & AI Usage

  • Machine Learning Algorithms – Optimizing analytics insights and reporting.
  • Fraud Detection & Security Screening – Identifying unauthorized activities.
  • User Experience Personalization – Customizing interface based on behavioral patterns.

Users may opt out of certain automated data processing features via privacy settings.

5. Cross-Border Data Compliance & Transfers

As a global service, personal data may be stored or processed outside Malaysia. We ensure compliance with:

  • Malaysia’s Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR - EU)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA - U.S.)
  • Other applicable regional privacy regulations

For international transfers, we implement:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data compliance.
  • Data localization policies when legally required.
  • Encryption and secure transfer protocols to protect user data.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data but may share information under specific conditions:

  • Third-Party Platforms – Users direct content to integrated platforms (e.g., YouTube, Facebook).
  • Service Providers – Hosting, security, and payment processing partners.
  • Legal Compliance – Meeting Malaysian regulatory requirements when required by law.

7. Data Storage & Security Measures

We apply industry-leading security protocols to safeguard personal data:

  • End-to-End Encryption – Securing passwords and payment details.
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) – Protecting accounts against unauthorized access.
  • Routine Security Audits – Identifying vulnerabilities and strengthening defenses.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain user data only for as long as required:

A. Personal Information & Accounts

  • Retained until account closure or five years after last activity, whichever occurs first.
  • Users may request deletion unless legally required for retention.

B. Uploaded Content & Metadata

  • Retained as long as the user maintains storage in the Service.
  • Deleted content is removed within 30 days upon user request.

C. Analytics & Usage Metrics

  • Aggregated analytics retained for five years for business insights.
  • Personalized tracking data stored for one year, unless legally required.

D. Payment & Transaction Records

  • Retained for seven years for tax compliance and auditing purposes.

E. Security Logs & Backups

  • Security logs retained for two years.
  • Database backups retained for 60 days before deletion.

F. Legal & Compliance Obligations

Certain data may be retained beyond standard periods if required by Malaysian law.

9. Data Portability Rights & Restrictions

Users have the right to request a copy of their personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format for portability purposes:

  • Account Information – Name, email, registration details.
  • Uploaded Content Metadata – Titles, descriptions, timestamps.
  • Basic Usage Metrics – Video upload history, login records.
  • Requests will be processed within 30 days, unless restricted by law or security concerns.
  • Data will be provided in CSV, JSON, or XML format for interoperability.

A. Restrictions on Proprietary Analytics

Certain data is classified as Company-owned intellectual property and is not eligible for portability, including:

  • AI-Generated Insights – Advanced analytics and behavioral predictions derived through internal algorithms.
  • Aggregated Platform-Wide Data – Trend analysis and usage benchmarks.
  • Algorithmic Scoring & Metrics – User engagement, optimization, and predictive insights.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention Logs – System-generated data used for fraud detection.

B. Limitations on Third-Party Data Portability

Portability rights do not extend to data originating from third-party platforms, including:

  • Analytics from external services (e.g., YouTube, Facebook).
  • User engagement data managed by external providers.
  • Licensed content stored under third-party agreements.

Users seeking portability for third-party-generated data must request access directly from the respective platforms.
